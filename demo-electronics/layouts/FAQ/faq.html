Orders & Returns

How do I return an item?

From the My Orders page, select the order you wish to return and click "Return order". We will create a UPS shipping label for you to take to any UPS Store and ship your return.

How do I track my order?

When an order is shipped, we will send an email with tracking information to the email address provided in the order. You can also navigate to the My Orders page and click on your order to see the tracking information.
